# Virtual Inventory Management

Stockbud's core power lies in its ability to centralize inventory across different types of stores.

## Unified Product Catalog

When you connect a Shopify store, your products are imported into the Stockbud **Master Catalog**. You can then link these products to your Social Stores.

## Stock Deductions

- **Shopify Sale**: When an order is paid on Shopify, Stockbud automatically deducts stock from the master catalog and updates all other linked stores.
- **Manual Social Sale**: When you mark a WhatsApp order as "Paid", Stockbud deducts the stock and syncs the new level back to Shopify via API.

## Safety Stock

You can set **Safety Stock** levels for each channel to prevent overselling on high-velocity social platforms like Instagram during a flash sale.
