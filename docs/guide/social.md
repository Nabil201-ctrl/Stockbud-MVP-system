# Social Stores

Stockbud allows you to manage non-Shopify stores, like WhatsApp and Instagram sellers, as first-class storefronts.

## Creating a Social Store

1. Navigate to **Stores** in the dashboard.
2. Click **Add Store > Social Store**.
3. Select the platform (WhatsApp or Instagram).
4. Give your store a name and set the currency.

## Managing Orders

Since social stores don't have automatic webhooks, you can manually enter orders received via DM or chat:
- Click **Quick Order** (+ icon).
- Select the products discussed.
- Add the customer's social handle.
- Mark as "Awaiting Payment" or "Paid".

## WhatsApp Sharing

Once an order is created, you can generate a **WhatsApp Share Link**. This sends a professional order summary to your customer with a payment link, ensuring a seamless checkout experience even on chat platforms.
